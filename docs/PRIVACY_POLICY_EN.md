# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 6, 2025**

"OtakuMap" (also known as "圣地巡礼", hereinafter referred to as "the App") is developed and maintained by an individual developer. We respect and protect user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Information

- **What we collect**: Your device's geographic location (precise or approximate)
- **Purpose**: To display your current location on the pilgrimage map and show distances to anime locations
- **How we collect**: Only when you actively use the map feature and grant location permission
- **Storage**: Location data is used temporarily during app usage only. **It is NOT saved or uploaded to any server.**

### 1.2 Local Favorites Data

- **What we collect**: Information about anime and locations you bookmark (anime ID, name, cover image URL, bookmark time, location coordinates, etc.)
- **Purpose**: To provide you with bookmark and management features
- **Storage**: All bookmark data is stored **locally on your device only** and is NOT uploaded to any server.

### 1.3 Network Request Data

- **Data sources**: The App retrieves anime and location information from the following public third-party APIs:
  - Bangumi (bgm.tv)
  - Anitabi (anitabi.cn)
- **Note**: We only retrieve publicly available anime information. **We do NOT collect or upload any personal user information.**

---

## 2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

1. **Provide core features**: Display anime information, pilgrimage maps, bookmark management
2. **Improve user experience**: Show nearby pilgrimage spots on the map based on your location
3. **Local data storage**: Save your bookmarks for future use

## 4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

- All network requests use HTTPS encrypted transmission
- User data is stored locally on the device only, not uploaded to servers
- The App does not require user registration or login, and does not collect account information

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Local data**: You can delete bookmarked anime and locations at any time within the App
- **Complete deletion**: Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data
- **Location data**: Not retained; only temporarily accessed during use

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Right to access**: View all your bookmarked anime and location information
- **Right to delete**: Delete your bookmark data at any time
- **Right to withdraw consent**: Revoke location permission in system settings at any time
